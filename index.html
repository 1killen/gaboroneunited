Gaborone United Football Club, also known as The Reds or GU, is one of Botswana’s most historic and successful football clubs. Founded in 1967, the club is based in Gaborone, the capital city of Botswana. With a legacy of excellence both on and off the field, Gaborone United has become a household name in Botswana Premier League football.

Over the decades, GU has built a reputation for producing talented players, playing attacking football, and maintaining high professional standards. The club boasts a passionate fan base and has consistently competed at the highest levels of domestic and continental competitions.

Gaborone United is proud of its achievements, which include:

  • Multiple Botswana Premier League Championships
  • Mascom Top 8 and FA Cup victories
  • Regular participation in CAF Confederation and Champions League tournaments

The club's mission is to elevate football in Botswana by developing young talent, engaging the community, and striving for excellence on the pitch. With strong leadership, modern facilities, and an ambitious vision, Gaborone United is more than just a club — it's a legacy in motion.

Club Colors: Red and White
