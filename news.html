Mpho Kgaswane Shines Bright in Midfield

May 2025 — Gaborone United’s midfield maestro, Mpho Kgaswane, continues to impress with his dynamic play and vision on the field. After joining the club in 2022, Kgaswane has become an irreplaceable part of the starting XI. Known for his pace, passing, and ability to unlock defenses, fans have dubbed him the “engine of GU.” His recent assist against Jwaneng Galaxy is already being replayed in highlight reels across Botswana.

Lebogang Ditsile: The Wall at the Back

May 2025 — Lebogang Ditsile, Gaborone United’s rock-solid defender, has been instrumental in keeping clean sheets this season. His commanding presence, aerial dominance, and leadership have helped shape one of the league’s strongest defenses. A product of Botswana’s youth development pipeline, Ditsile brings both experience and passion to the backline. “Defending is an art,” he says — and he’s painting a masterpiece this season.
